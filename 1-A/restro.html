Our Story

Ristorante Bella Vita was founded in 1985 by Chef Antonio Rossi, who brought his family recipes from the small coastal town of Positano, Italy to create an authentic Italian dining experience.

For over three decades, our restaurant has been serving traditional Italian cuisine made with the freshest ingredients and prepared with passion and care. Our commitment to quality and tradition has made us a beloved destination for Italian food enthusiasts.

Today, Chef Antonio's son Marco continues the family legacy, adding his own modern twist to classic Italian dishes while preserving the authentic flavors that have made Bella Vita famous.
